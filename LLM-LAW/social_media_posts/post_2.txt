Post 2 — Why Multilingual Embeddings Matter

When building a RAG system for Greek documents, one of the biggest mistakes is using English‑only embeddings.

A much better approach:
Use multilingual models like:

intfloat/multilingual-e5-base

Why it matters:
• Handles Greek legal text well
• Works with mixed Greek–English documents
• Produces much better semantic search results

In the system I built:
Greek PDFs → embeddings → FAISS vector search → Meltemi‑7B answers grounded in context.

Inspired by workflows from the Coursera course “ChatGPT Code Interpreter” by Jules White.
