Post 3 — Turning Documents Into a Knowledge Assistant

With a simple RAG pipeline you can transform internal documents into an intelligent assistant.

Example use cases:
• Laws and regulations
• HR manuals
• Academic papers
• Compliance documentation
• Internal policies

Workflow:

1. Load documents (PDF, DOCX, TXT)
2. Chunk them into small sections
3. Create embeddings
4. Store vectors in FAISS
5. Retrieve relevant sections
6. Inject them into the LLM prompt

Meltemi‑7B then generates answers grounded in the retrieved content.

The result: high accuracy and dramatically reduced hallucinations.
